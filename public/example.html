Integration Instructions

To integrate VertoAD into your website, follow these simple steps:

1. Add the VertoAD script to your page

<script src="https://your-domain.com/embed.js"></script>

2. Place ad containers where you want ads to appear

<div class="verto-ad" data-size="300x250"></div>

Available Ad Sizes

  • Medium Rectangle: 300x250
  • Leaderboard: 728x90
  • Wide Skyscraper: 160x600
